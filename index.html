CPG Deck-builder

What is it?

CPG Deck-Builder has no official name yet, but is planned to be a Completely Procedurally Generated form of popular games like Slay the Spire and Rogue Adventures.

This game is not intended to compete with other roguelite deck-builders, and will not have good graphics, as it is coded in Python. ¯\_(ツ)_/¯
